Dead Angles Institute

Dead Angles Institute is an independent research space dedicated to exploring intellectual, cultural and historical blind spots. Our work consists in examining neglected, obscure or deliberately excluded subjects from public debate.

Through the journal Civilization or Barbarism, we publish long-form investigations devoted to these shadow areas. Each text aims less to conclude than to reopen questions that our time often prefers to avoid.

The objective is not to produce rapid opinion, but to build a lasting critical archive.

Why PDF format?

We deliberately chose the PDF format. In a world shaped by speed and constant scrolling, certain forms of writing call for slowing down.

PDF invites a different relationship to text — one that values attention, reflection and continuity.

Our articles are designed to be read calmly, printed and preserved, annotated by hand, and shared in educational or discussion contexts.

These are not texts for the moment. They are texts meant to endure.
